## Primary Research Focus

### Soil-Ecosystem Material Cycling and Regulation

**Core Research Areas:**
- Soil nutrient cycling mechanisms and their regulatory factors
- Carbon-nitrogen-phosphorus coupling and synergistic management
- Solute transport and soil-water interaction processes
- Ecosystem carbon budget quantification and uncertainty analysis

---

## Specific Research Topics

### Contaminant Transport and Fate in Soil-Water Systems

- Heavy metal mobility and bioavailability in soil profiles
- Organic pollutant sorption and transformation in soil matrices
- Nutrient leaching and runoff losses under extreme conditions
- Transport modeling in heterogeneous soil systems

---

### Forest and Agricultural Ecosystem Management

- Carbon sequestration potential in forest soils
- Sustainable management of forest biomass and nutrient cycling
- Agricultural byproduct application effects on soil properties
- Ecosystem service valuation and optimization

---

### Environmental Monitoring and Assessment

- Greenhouse gas emission mechanisms from soils
- Microbial community structure and functional diversity
- Water quality assessment and remediation strategies
- Environmental risk assessment methodologies

---

## Methodological Interests

- Advanced analytical techniques (isotope tracing, mass spectrometry)
- Geospatial analysis and remote sensing applications
- Statistical modeling and multivariate analysis
- GIS-based ecosystem assessment and mapping

---

*Research interests continue to evolve with new discoveries in environmental science and sustainability challenges.*
